Our Team

We are a diverse group of students from Creative Media Design (CMD) and Creative Media and Game Technology (CMGT) at the Hogeschool Rotterdam. United by our passion for enhancing elderly care through innovative solutions, our team blends expertise in design, technology, and user experience. Each of us brings a unique set of skills and perspectives, driving forward our commitment to developing accessible and engaging technologies that promote active lifestyles among seniors. Get to know the creative minds behind our fall prevention initiative!
